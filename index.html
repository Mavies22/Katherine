About Us

Welcome to Paws & Claws by Mavies, where your pet’s well-being is our passion. Since 2024, we’ve been a trusted platform connecting pet owners with top-notch veterinary shops, experienced professionals, and a wide range of services designed to keep your furry friends happy and healthy.


What we can do for you

Loving Care, Tailored for Your Pet

We understand how much your pets mean to you. Our compassionate and experienced staff ensure that every pet is treated with the utmost love and respect, just like family. At Paws & Claws by Mavies, we’re not just a service — we’re your partner in pet care. Let us help you give your pets the life they deserve.

Expert Health Solutions for Your Pets

Our skilled veterinarians provide thorough medical care and personalized advice to keep your pets thriving. Whether it’s routine wellness checks or specialized treatments, we’ve got you covered.

Pamper Your Pet with Premium Grooming

From luxurious baths to stylish cuts, our professional grooming services will leave your pet looking and feeling fabulous. Because every pet deserves a little extra care.
